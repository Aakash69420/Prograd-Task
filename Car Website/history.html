Our History

AAKARS Cars Company was founded in 1955 by John Aakars in a small garage in Detroit, Michigan. Aakars had a passion for cars and dreamed of building the best cars in the world. He started out by designing and building custom cars for wealthy clients, but soon realized that he could make a bigger impact by building cars for the masses.

In 1958, AAKARS Cars Company introduced its first mass-produced car, the AAKARS 1000. The car was an instant success and quickly became known for its innovative design and superior performance. Over the next decade, AAKARS Cars Company continued to innovate and introduced several new models, including the AAKARS 2000 and the AAKARS 3000.

In the 1970s, AAKARS Cars Company faced tough competition from foreign automakers and struggled to keep up with changing market trends. The company went through a series of ownership changes and financial difficulties, but managed to survive thanks to its loyal fan base and innovative designs.

Today, AAKARS Cars Company is one of the most respected names in the automotive industry, known for its cutting-edge technology and stylish designs. The company continues to push the boundaries of what's possible in the world of cars, and its legacy lives on as a testament to John Aakars' vision and determination.
